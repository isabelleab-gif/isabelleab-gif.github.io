Peer Assistance for Learning Application Flyer

Every year, the Peer Assistance for Learning program requires a new application flyer, as we try to build stronger and stronger pools of applicants. In my first year as a designer for the PAL program, I helped implement and advertise a flyer that brought our applicants from 17 to 96 in one academic year!

Community Resiliency Model Bike Flyer Redesign

This is a design I'm especially proud of. The Community Resiliency Model is a stress response model that strives to help people, especially students, regulate their nervous systems. As part of the Cook Counseling Center's outreach program, us PALs take a motorized bike filled with fidget toys to a busy spot on campus. We try to reach as many students as possible, offering free fidget toys and bending their ear about Counseling Center services. This poster was designed to go with the bike, meant to catch the eyes of more students.

"Make Your Own Stress Balls" Promotional Flyer

This is my most recent design. While it was a last minute project compared to others I've been assigned, this is one where I got to play around with colors outside of VT's brand book. Again, my focus was catching the eyes of students who might be interesting in taking a hands-on approach to stress relief by joining a stress-ball-making workshop.

El Centro Cultural Community Center x PALs Event
